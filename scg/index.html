What is Senior Class Gift 2015 ?

IIT-KGP has a long and historic legacy of Alumni Giving Back which has existed in one or the other form. The Graduating Class Gift is yet another chapter to be added in KGP alumni legacy initiated under My Imprint Program.

It is a class-based effort directed towards raising unrestricted fund partially used currently and partially going towards Endowment. This particular part of the Endowment would again be utilized for students' services only. It is an opportunity for graduating students to take their first step in being soon-to-be alumni by making their Graduating Class Gift, the gift which impacts student life in the KGP campus. This challenge offers seniors the chance not only to make a gift, but also to leave behind their imprint.

Mechanism to select Senior Class Gift 2015

Committee members freeze 9 options for Senior Class Gift, 3 options in each range
These 9 options go to final year students for open voting
Highest voted option in each range will be considered as anticipated Senior Class Gift 2015
At the end of the campaign, Senior Class Gift 2015 would be decided based on the amount collected by the Graduating Batch and would be announced in Alvida in the month of April

Campus Benches

The class can donate 10 stone and wood benches placed throughout 2.2. Designed to be permanent fixtures, this will look elegant and prominent all around 2.2

Bus Stands

This can be the most useful option for students. This will be designed in a manner to have facilitation for branding and promotion. Minimum 10 stands would be required all around the campus

Literary Section

New library shelves dedicated to literary fiction (shelves along with books as class gift)

Grand Pillar Clock

Class may gift this unique Street Clock, preferably on Gol Chakkar “Tikka” , which will be recognized as a landmark for 2015 Batch and will become a historical monument in coming years.

Water Fountain

A water fountain with 2015 placed on top of a stone/marble structure in the middle of it

Science Center

A centre for preservation of innovative projects undertaken by KGPians (Tech GC in particular).
We see a lot of innovative ideas come to reality in various Tech Events throughout the year. Most of these ideas go unharnessed and are lost in the sands of time. The main aim is to preserve as well as show case the talent in research and help them in recognition via patent.

Art Gallery

KGP Campus is flooded with artistic talent but no place to preserve it and showcase it for the long. The batch can come up with the set up of Art Gallery which would provide a platform to showcase talents related to arts, paintings, photos and other innovation

Celebration Center

A celebration centre where students can celebrate birthdays, etc (should have necessary facilities)

Music Recording Studio

It would be the most desirable gift for the students passionate for music. Also it can be used for Institute recording projects.

Brand Value Of IIT Kgp

Your gift would help the Institute in progressing towards being in World's top 20 technological Institute [IIT-KGP VISION 2020]. Institute Ranking increases the value of degrees and so the brand value of campus students as well as alumni across the globe.

Importance of Endowment

Institute's alumni giving rate is an important measure which is used to rank and evaluate IIT-KGP and there is an 80% correlation between World's Top Ranking Institution and their Endowment size.

Alma Mater that changed your lives forever

IIT-KGP has provided you with outstanding, academic resources, lasting friendships and countless memories over the years you spent at KGP campus. Now it is your chance to pay tribute to your Alma Mater that changed your lives forever. Senior Class Gift provides you with the 1st chance to give back to Alma Mater to enrich the students' experience of future generation.
